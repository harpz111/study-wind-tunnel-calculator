Study Wind Tunnel Calculator

A preliminary sizing tool for open-circuit, low-speed wind tunnels, intended for students, hobbyists, and universities performing early-stage feasibility and layout studies.

What this tool does

  • Links all tunnel sections back to the test section
  • Computes local Mach number, Reynolds number, and friction factor
  • Applies empirical loss correlations from established wind-tunnel literature
  • Forms a total loss coefficient and energy ratio
  • Estimates total pressure drop and required fan power

Intended use

This tool is intended for rough preliminary sizing only. It is not a replacement for detailed design, CFD, or experimental validation.
