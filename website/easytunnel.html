EasyTunnel

Preliminary sizing tool for low-speed wind tunnels

Overview

This tool performs preliminary sizing of open-circuit, low-speed wind tunnels. It estimates losses, energy ratio, and required power based on test-section inputs.

Method

The tunnel is modelled as contraction, test section, and diffuser. Losses are calculated using empirical correlations and summed.

Assumptions

  • Low-speed, subsonic flow
  • Steady flow
  • Empirical loss correlations

Use

Intended for preliminary sizing and feasibility studies only.

Limitations

  • No fan curves
  • No detailed CFD
  • Approximate correlations
