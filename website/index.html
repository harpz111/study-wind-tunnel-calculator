About me

I am interested in aerospace engineering, fluid mechanics, computational fluid dynamics, numerical methods, and the wider challenge of understanding how physical systems behave.

I want this site to act as a personal base: somewhere I can keep my reading lists, technical notes, project links, and a clear record of what I am trying to learn and build over time.

Goals

  • Deepen my understanding of CFD, numerical methods, and fluid mechanics.
  • Build technically credible projects that demonstrate real engineering ability.
  • Keep organised notes on what I learn rather than letting ideas get lost.
  • Read more systematically, both technically and for enjoyment.
  • Create a website that reflects my interests and work clearly.
